What [Music Site] is:

How many times have you been at a party, in a car, or even just hanging out with friends, and the music playing absolutely sucks? Well, [Music Site] aims to fix that issue. If you use [Music App], the days of hearing songs nobody likes are over. By voting on what songs you want to hear, the most-wanted song will always be playing.
