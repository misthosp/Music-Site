What is GroupJam?

Name the last time you were at a party, get-together, or even just hanging out with friends, and the music playing was absolutely awful. Probably not that long ago.

With [Music App], the days of crappy DJs are over. How can we eliminate such a timeless plague on party-goers everywhere, you ask? Simple: we simply make everybody the DJ. All the host of the party has to do is create a [group] and have guests join the group. From there, guests can both suggest and vote on songs. Whichever song has the most votes by the time the current song ends, plays next. That way, it's a guarantee that songs people want to hear will always be playing. Easy as that!

So kiss the days of hearing Sweet Caroline at a rager or Shots at a barbecue goodbye! Use [Music App], and let your party be its own DJ!
